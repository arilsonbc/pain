Agricultural Forecasting: Rainfall Impact on Corn Prices

In agribusiness, understanding the temporal precedence between weather and price is what sets a common analysis apart from a market strategy. This project evolved from an automated ETL pipeline to a complete econometric model that quantifies the real impact of rainfall in the Passo Fundo/RS region.

Architecture technical highlights:

  • Collection & Ingestion: Web scraping (Not√≠cias Agr√≠colas) and API integration (Open-Meteo) to consolidate price and weather time series.

  • Orchestration: Daily automation and pipeline health monitoring via GitHub Actions.
  • Econometric Analysis: Application of Granger Causality Test and OLS (Ordinary Least Squares) regression to isolate variables.
  • Visualization: Dynamic dashboard in Looker Studio integrated with a Forecasting model for the next 6 days.

Through modeling, a 6-day logistic Lag was identified: the regional physical market takes almost a week to fully absorb the supply shocks caused by intense rainfall.
